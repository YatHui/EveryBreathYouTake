Behind the scenes

Here you will find out how we made all this possible.

Why?

Sustainability is arguably the most crucial issue facing humanity today. Visualisation makes it easy to make sense of the data so we can make data-driven decisions. We want to highlight the data behind something many take for granted: clean air that is safe to breathe!

How?

We obtained datasets from reputable sources like the World Health Organization and Our World In Data. Once we have extracted and transformed the data to tell our story, the data is then stored in the cloud on an AWS Relational Database.

Then...

...we pulled from the database to visualise this health and air quality data with interactive graphs. We use these graphs to create data stories on a website developed using Flask and hosted on AWS EC2.

The Team

We are four students on the AW Academy’s 12 weeks’s intensive course in Data Engineering. This is our graduation project which we made in the last two weeks of the course. Check out our presentation and repo on GitHub.
