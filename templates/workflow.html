Behind the scenes

Here you will find out how we made all this possible.

Why?

Sustainability is arguably the most crucial issue facing humanity today. We aim to highlight the data behind something many take for granted: clean air that is safe to breathe!

How?

Initially, we obtained datasets from reputable sources like the World Health Organization and Our World In Data. This information was then stored in an AWS Relational Database in the cloud.

Then...

...we harmonized the data and created interactive graphs for visualizing the information. Along with comprehensive explanatory details, everything was consolidated on a website developed using Flask.
