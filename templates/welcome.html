How air pollution affects our health

The health effects of air pollution are serious. Air pollution is the fourth biggest cause of death - after high blood pressure, tobacco use, and poor diet. Air pollution is hard to escape from as it is all around us. Air pollution

Air pollution is the contamination of air due to substances in the atmosphere that are harmful to the health of humans and other living beings. These pollutants also damage the built and natural environment.
